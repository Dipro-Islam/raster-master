Entrepreneurship and Innovation Training

"Entrepreneurship is living a few years of your life like most people won’t so you can spend the rest of your life like most people can’t." — Anonymous

Impact:
The Entrepreneurship and Innovation Training program has sparked creativity and innovation in young minds, with over 60% of participants launching their own small businesses or startups. The program empowers youth with the knowledge and tools to create sustainable enterprises, helping to lift families out of poverty and drive economic growth in their communities.
