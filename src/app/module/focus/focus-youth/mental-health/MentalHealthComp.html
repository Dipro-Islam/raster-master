Mental Health and Well-Being Support

"You don’t have to control your thoughts. You just have to stop letting them control you." — Dan Millman

Impact:
The Mental Health and Well-Being Support program has significantly improved emotional resilience among participants, with 90% of youth reporting better stress management and emotional health. By offering counseling services, peer support, and mental health workshops, the program helps young people build resilience and prioritize their mental well-being in an increasingly stressful world.
