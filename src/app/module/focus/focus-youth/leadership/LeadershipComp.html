Leadership and Life Skills Development

"Leadership is not about being in charge. It’s about taking responsibility for those in your care." — Simon Sinek

Impact:
Through the Leadership and Life Skills Development program, young participants have gained vital leadership abilities that prepare them to navigate real-world challenges. Over 80% of participants report improved decision-making skills, enhanced teamwork, and greater confidence in leading community initiatives. Many have gone on to lead local projects, mentor peers, and take active roles in improving their communities.
