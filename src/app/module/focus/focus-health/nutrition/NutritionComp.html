Nutrition

To address the unique health challenges faced by underprivileged mothers, our organization arranges free health check-ups as our support initiatives. Partnering with community based clinics and healthcare providers, help us mitigate the limited healthcare constraints, financial barriers, prenatal, and postnatal barriers. These health check-ups are conducted regularly and include a range of services, from routine examinations and screenings to nutritional counseling and mental health support. By bringing healthcare directly to the communities, we ensure that underprivileged mothers receive the essential care they need, empowering them to achieve optimal well-being for themselves and their families. This proactive approach not only improves immediate health outcomes but also fosters long-term health and resilience within these communities.
