Children First: Building a Better Tomorrow

At Raster Master’s Teacher of the Streets, our mission is to ensure that every child enjoys a happy, healthy, and secure childhood. According to the UN, over 200 million children globally do not have access to quality education, healthcare, or proper nutrition. By investing in their well-being, we are investing in a better world. Through our comprehensive programs, we are committed to providing children with the education, nutrition, and care they need to thrive. "Every child deserves the chance to reach their full potential. Together, we can build a world where no child is left behind."

Creating Joyful Beginnings: The Right to a Happy Childhood

A happy childhood is the foundation of a fulfilling life. According to the UN’s SDG 3, ensuring healthy lives and promoting well-being for all at all ages is essential for sustainable development. However, an estimated 150 million children worldwide suffer from malnutrition, affecting their physical and cognitive development. At Teacher of the Streets, we strive to create environments where children can laugh, play, and grow in safety and happiness. Our programs are designed to foster creativity, resilience, and a sense of belonging, giving every child the opportunity to experience the joy they deserve. "Childhood is the most beautiful of all life’s seasons. Let’s make every moment count."
