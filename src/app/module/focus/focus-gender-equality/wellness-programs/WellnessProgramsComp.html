Women’s Health and Wellness Programs

Quote: "When women are healthy, their families, communities, and nations thrive." — Hillary Clinton

Impact:
The Women’s Health and Wellness Programs provide access to critical health services, including reproductive health education, maternal care, and mental health support, to over 3,500 women annually. These initiatives have drastically improved health outcomes for women, reducing maternal mortality rates and increasing awareness about personal and family health. By prioritizing women’s health, we are building healthier families and more resilient communities.
