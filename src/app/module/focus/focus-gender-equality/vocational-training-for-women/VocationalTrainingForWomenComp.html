Through our Skill Development and Vocational Training programs, over 5,000 women in underprivileged communities have gained marketable skills in areas such as tailoring, handicrafts, and digital literacy. Many participants have successfully started their own small businesses, contributing to their family's income and achieving financial independence. By empowering women with skills, we are not only transforming individual lives but also fostering stronger, self-reliant communities.
