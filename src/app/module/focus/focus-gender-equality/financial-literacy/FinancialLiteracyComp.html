Impact:
The Financial Literacy and Microfinance Programs have equipped over 2,500 women with the knowledge to manage their finances, save for the future, and access microloans to start small businesses. Many participants have successfully utilized their new financial skills to escape the cycle of debt and poverty, building a more secure future for themselves and their families. This initiative has created a culture of economic empowerment, where women take control of their financial destinies.
