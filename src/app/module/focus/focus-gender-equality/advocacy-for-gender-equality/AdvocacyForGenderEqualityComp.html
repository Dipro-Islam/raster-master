Coastal and River Clean-Up Campaigns

"Keep close to Nature’s heart... and break clear away, once in a while, and climb a mountain or spend a week in the woods. Wash your spirit clean." — John Muir

Impact:
Our Coastal and River Clean-Up Campaigns have mobilized over 3,000 volunteers to remove more than 10 tons of waste from local rivers and coastal areas. This project has not only improved water quality and local ecosystems but has also raised awareness about the impact of pollution on aquatic life. Local communities have taken ownership of keeping their waterways clean, fostering a long-term commitment to environmental stewardship.
