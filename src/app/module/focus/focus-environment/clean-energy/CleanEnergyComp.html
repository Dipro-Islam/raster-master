Impact:
Through our Clean Energy and Solar Power Projects, we have installed solar panels in 10 schools and community centers, providing renewable energy to areas with limited access to electricity. This project has reduced reliance on non-renewable energy sources, saving an estimated 50 tons of carbon emissions annually. Communities now have access to clean, reliable energy, improving their quality of life while promoting sustainable energy practices.
