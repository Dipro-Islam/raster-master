Impact:
Our Water Conservation Initiatives have led to the installation of rainwater harvesting systems in 30 rural villages, ensuring access to clean water for over 5,000 families. By educating communities on efficient water use and introducing sustainable practices, water usage has decreased by 25%, significantly improving local water security and helping communities combat the effects of drought and water scarcity.
