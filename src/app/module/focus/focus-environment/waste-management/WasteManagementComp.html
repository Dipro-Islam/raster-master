Waste Management and Recycling Programs

"Waste isn’t waste until we waste it." — Will.i.am

Impact:
The Waste Management and Recycling Programs have successfully introduced sustainable waste disposal practices in over 50 communities. With the establishment of waste sorting systems and educational workshops on recycling, over 70% of participants have adopted new waste reduction habits. The project has significantly reduced waste sent to landfills, promoted the reuse of materials, and inspired participants to make environmentally responsible choices.
