Inclusive Skill Development Program for Youth with a Focus on Girls and Women

"Skills empower futures."

Our Inclusive Skill Development Program is dedicated to providing young people, particularly girls and women, with the skills they need to thrive in the workforce. From technical training in fields like digital literacy, renewable energy, and entrepreneurship to vocational skills in tailoring, handicrafts, and healthcare, this program will empower women and girls to be economically independent and contribute to their communities. Special focus will be given to bridging the gender gap in traditionally male-dominated industries.
