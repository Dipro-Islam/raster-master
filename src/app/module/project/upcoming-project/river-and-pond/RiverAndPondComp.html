River and Pond Cleanliness Project

"Clean rivers, healthy lives."

The River and Pond Cleanliness Project is designed to tackle water pollution and restore the health of local water bodies, which are vital sources of water for drinking, irrigation, and livelihoods. This project involves cleaning up rivers and ponds, preventing further pollution, and promoting awareness about the importance of maintaining clean water bodies. Through community participation, we aim to ensure that local ecosystems thrive and water quality is improved for all.
