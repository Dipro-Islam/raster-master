Harbour Renewal Project

"Reviving harbours, revitalizing economies."

Our Harbour Renewal Project focuses on the restoration and modernization of small and neglected harbours. These harbours play a vital role in local economies, supporting fishing, trade, and transportation. By renewing these harbours, we aim to boost local livelihoods, enhance coastal tourism, and promote sustainable economic growth in the region. The project will also ensure that environmental best practices are followed to protect marine ecosystems.
