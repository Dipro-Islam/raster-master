Rural Infrastructure Development: Sanitation and Toilets

Sanitation is dignity.

Improving sanitation is crucial for public health, especially in rural areas. Our Rural Infrastructure Development project will focus on building toilets and improving sanitation in underserved villages, aiming to reduce open defecation and provide access to clean and safe sanitation facilities. This project will prioritize schools and public spaces, ensuring that communities, especially women and children, have access to dignified, hygienic spaces
