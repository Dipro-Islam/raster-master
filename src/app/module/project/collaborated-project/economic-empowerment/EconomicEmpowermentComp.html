Women’s Economic Empowerment Program

The Women’s Economic Empowerment Program, our collaboration with microfinance institutions and local cooperatives supports SDG 5—Gender Equality and SDG 8—Decent Work and Economic Growth. This partnership provided 5,000 women with the financial resources, training, and mentorship they needed to start their own businesses. As a result, these women have seen their household incomes increase by an average of 30%, and community-wide economic participation has grown significantly.
