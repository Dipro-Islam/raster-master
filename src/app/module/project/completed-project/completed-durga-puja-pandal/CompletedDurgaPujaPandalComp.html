Durga Puja Pandal: Celebrating Culture and Unity

In celebration of Durga Puja, Rastar Master sponsored and organized a traditional pandal, creating a sacred space for community worship, celebration, and unity. The pandal brought together families and friends, reinforcing cultural bonds and offering a reprieve from daily hardships. Although no longer active, this project fostered a sense of belonging and cultural pride, bringing the community closer in times of celebration and faith.

"In celebrating our traditions, we honor our past and build a more united future."
