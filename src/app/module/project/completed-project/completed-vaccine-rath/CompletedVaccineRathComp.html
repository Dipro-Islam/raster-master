Vaccine Rath: Health on Wheels

Inspired by the tradition of the Rath Yatra, Rastar Master launched the Vaccine Rath, a mobile vaccination drive that brought vaccines to the doorsteps of remote and underserved communities. By making vaccinations accessible to those unable to reach health centers, this initiative bridged a critical healthcare gap, fostering health equity. The Vaccine Rath encapsulates Rastar Master’s mission to overcome barriers, offering life-saving resources to all who need them. Though completed, it remains a powerful reminder of the role that accessible healthcare plays in creating resilient communities. "When care travels to you, it becomes more than a service; it’s a commitment to life and health."
