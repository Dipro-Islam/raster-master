General Information

Raster Master was inspired by a vision to provide quality education to marginalized communities, focusing on indigenous and disadvantaged village students, particularly girls. Our mission is to ensure education for all, eliminating biases and fostering an inclusive learning environment.

We engage students through interactive digital platforms, hands-on projects, extracurricular activities, and community involvement, making learning fun and meaningful.

Our unique approach combines the 3G model of intergenerational learning, a strong focus on digital literacy, and comprehensive support for underprivileged communities, particularly girls, ensuring holistic development.

Educational Programs

The 3G model strengthens family bonds by involving mothers and grandmothers in the learning process, creating a supportive environment and encouraging lifelong learning within families.

We use a blend of digital tools, interactive simulations, project-based learning, and gamification to make education engaging and effective for our students

Self-defense classes empower female students by boosting their confidence, teaching them valuable safety skills, and promoting physical fitness and mental resilience.

Digital Literacy and ICT

Students are introduced to tools such as educational apps, coding platforms, virtual labs, and collaborative online spaces to enhance their digital literacy and technical skills.

We conduct regular workshops on cybersecurity, teach responsible internet usage, and provide resources to help students and parents understand online safety practices.

Digital platforms facilitate continuous assessments through interactive quizzes, online assignments, and real-time feedback, helping track student progress efficiently.
