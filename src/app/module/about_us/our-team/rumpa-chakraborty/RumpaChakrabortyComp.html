Rumpa Chakraborty

Researcher of “Teacher of the Street”

Our Researcher

*Rumpa Chakraborty* is a passionate educator, researcher, and librarian dedicated to fostering learning and literacy within marginalized communities. As a valued member of the *Raster Master International Foundation*, she plays a crucial role in advancing community-based research under the visionary guidance of Chief Researcher Deep Narayan Nayak.

Her work aligns with Raster Master’s mission to empower underserved populations through education, resource accessibility, and literacy programs. Rumpa’s research focuses on innovative educational practices that enhance learning environments, support intergenerational learning, and bridge literacy gaps. Her diverse skill set and dedication to community engagement embody the spirit of *Raster Master*, contributing to transformative change for thousands of children and families.

As a librarian, she further enriches her impact by curating and sharing valuable resources that promote lifelong learning and educational equity
