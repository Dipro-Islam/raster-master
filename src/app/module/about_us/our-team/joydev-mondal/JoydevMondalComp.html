Joydev Mondal

Researcher of “Teacher of the Street”

Our Researcher

Joydev Mondal is a dedicated researcher at Raster Master, bringing a wealth of life experience as both a father and a grandfather. His extensive involvement with family and community gives him a unique perspective on the challenges faced by marginalized groups, especially in rural areas.

While his formal academic background may be limited, Joydev’s practical knowledge, gained through years of direct engagement with his community, enhances his research. His work focuses on identifying and addressing the educational needs of underserved populations, striving to improve learning outcomes for children and adults alike. Guided by the mentorship of Chief Researcher Deep Narayan Nayak, Joydev Mondal continues to contribute valuable insights, promoting inclusive education and social empowerment.
