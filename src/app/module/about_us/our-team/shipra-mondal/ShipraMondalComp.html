Shipra Mondal

Researcher of “Teacher of the Street”

Our Researcher

Shipra Mondal, a Ph.D. researcher in the Department of History at Jadavpur University, WB, India, is also an accomplished independent research scholar at Raster Master. With a strong academic foundation in history, Shipra brings valuable perspectives to the organization’s efforts in educational development and community empowerment. Her research focuses on understanding the historical context of marginalized communities and how these narratives shape their current educational needs and challenges.

Shipra’s work with Raster Master aims to bridge historical knowledge with contemporary educational practices, ensuring inclusive and transformative learning for underserved populations. Guided by the visionary leadership of Chief Researcher Deep Narayan Nayak, Shipra Mondal continues to contribute impactful, evidence-based research that supports Raster Master’s mission of advancing social change and fostering accessible education for all.
