Our Founder

"The Visionary Behind the Mission"

Deep Narayan Nayak, known as the "Teacher of the Streets," is the founder, visionary changemaker, and Director of Research and Development at the Raster Master International Foundation. He is a passionate educator, researcher, social worker, and advocate for marginalized communities. With over 15 years of experience, he leads Raster Master Project initiatives like the 3G, 3D, 5L, and MLS Learning Models and the Raster Master Democratic Research Model, which bring education, food, healthcare, equality, and well-being to more than 15,000 children across 100+ villages. His mottos, "Where there is a Wall, there is a Way," "One dollar can change 100 lives," and "Mothers & Grandmothers: The Heartbeat of Real Change," reflect his commitment to breaking the cycle of illiteracy and poverty.

Nayak's transformative work has received national and international accolades, including the Beautiful Indian Award and Sankalp Ratna Award (2024), as well as recognition as a Global Teacher Prize Finalist (2023), Global Teacher Award (2022), and Best Teacher Award (2020). His work has been featured in publications like Reuters, BBC, Voice of America, ABP NDTV, and many more.
