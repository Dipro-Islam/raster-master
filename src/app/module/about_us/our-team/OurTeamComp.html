Our Team

"A Team Driven by Passion and Purpose"

At Teacher of the Streets, our team is united by a shared commitment to creating positive change in the world. We believe that the power of a dedicated group of individuals, working together toward a common goal, can achieve the extraordinary. From our founders and advisors to our teachers and volunteers, each team member plays a crucial role in driving our mission forward. Our diverse team, with expertise in education, health, sustainability, and community development, allows us to address the complex challenges faced by the communities we serve. "It takes a team to build a dream."

"In every classroom, clinic, and community center, our team is there—empowering, educating, and inspiring."
