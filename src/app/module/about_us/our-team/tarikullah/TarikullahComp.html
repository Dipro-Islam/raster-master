Md. Tarikullah

Researcher of “Teacher of the Street”

Our Researcher

Md. Tarikullah is an accomplished Assistant Professor of Physics in Bangladesh, holding a B.Sc. (Honours) and an M.Sc. in Physics. With a strong commitment to education, he serves as a Master Trainer in Life Skill-Based Education, Practical Science, and Continuous Assessment, equipping educators with essential tools to enhance student learning outcomes. As a researcher with Raster Master.

Tarikullah focuses on integrating innovative teaching methodologies that promote practical engagement and critical thinking among students. His research is guided by the visionary leadership of Deep Narayan Nayak, under whose mentorship he explores effective educational practices that address the unique challenges faced by learners in marginalized communities.

Md. Tarikullah’s work emphasizes the importance of life skills and practical applications of science in fostering a holistic educational experience, enabling students to develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills essential for success in the 21st century. His dedication to educational development and his passion for empowering students position him as a key contributor to Raster Master’s mission of creating transformative learning environments that inspire and uplift communities.
