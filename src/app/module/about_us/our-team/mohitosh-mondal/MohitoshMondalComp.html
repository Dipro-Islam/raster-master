Mohitosh Mondal

Researcher of “Teacher of the Street”

Our Researcher

Mohitosh Mondal, an MA and Ph.D. researcher in the Department of History at Tilka Manjhi Bhagalpur University, is also an esteemed researcher with Raster Master, contributing his academic expertise to the organization’s mission of fostering education and community development. With a strong background in historical research, Mohitosh brings critical insights into understanding the socio-cultural dynamics of marginalized communities.

His work with Raster Master focuses on integrating historical perspectives into the development of inclusive education strategies, exploring how historical narratives can shape contemporary educational practices. Guided by the leadership of Chief Researcher Deep Narayan Nayak, Mohitosh Mondal continues to play a pivotal role in advancing impactful research that empowers underserved communities and promotes sustainable change.
