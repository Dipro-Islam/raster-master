Dr. Jhuma Patra

Researcher of “Teacher of the Street”

Our Researcher

Dr. Jhuma Patra is a distinguished researcher at Raster Master, holding a Ph.D. in Bengali Language and Literature, and brings a wealth of expertise in the intricacies of language and culture. She is dedicated to exploring the rich tapestry of Bengali literature and its relevance in contemporary education. In addition to her specialization.

Dr. Patra possesses diverse knowledge in educational research and development, allowing her to connect linguistic insights with effective educational practices that foster a deeper appreciation for the arts in learning environments. Actively engaged in innovative research initiatives, her work emphasizes culturally relevant pedagogy, empowering students to connect with their heritage while developing critical thinking and analytical skills.

Driven by a passion for educational equity, Dr. Patra collaborates with fellow researchers and educators to implement inclusive learning strategies, positioning her as a vital contributor to Raster Master’s mission of fostering transformative and impactful learning experiences.
