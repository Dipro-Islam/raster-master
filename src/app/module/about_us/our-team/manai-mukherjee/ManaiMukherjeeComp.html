Manai Mukherjee

Researcher of “Teacher of the Street”

Our Researcher

*Manai Mukherjee* is a dedicated mother, teacher, and researcher whose work with *Raster Master* projects is driven by compassion and a profound understanding of marginalized communities' needs. Under the guidance of Research Director Deep Narayan Nayak, she combines the precision of a researcher with the intuitive insight of a mother, embodying the motto: "Where researchers measure, mothers know; where policymakers plan, grandmothers sow."

Manai’s research focuses on creating inclusive, accessible, and culturally grounded learning environments, empowering mothers and grandmothers as core pillars of community knowledge and strength. Her commitment to the *Raster Master* mission reflects a deep belief in the transformative power of education and intergenerational learning to break cycles of poverty and illiteracy.
