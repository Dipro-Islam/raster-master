Our Advisor

Ajai Shreevatsa MBBS, MD, MBA.Advisor "I am honored to be involved in the work Deep and his team are doing. The length and breadth of reach of their work and the efficiency they are able to achieve is commendable and inspiring."

Dr. Ajai Shreevatsa, is a Medical Doctor who is in the University of North Carolina (UNC) health system in the Research Triangle area of North Carolina. He completed his medical school from Kempegowda Institute of Medical Sciences in Bengaluru, India. He then completed his residency in Internal Medicine from the Celeveland Clinic Health system in Cleveland, Ohio, United States. He will complete his MBA from UNC Wilmington, United States in December 2024. He currently works as a hospitalist and Physician Advisor in the University of North Carolina (UNC) health system in the Research Triangle area of North Carolina. He has worked in various clinical settings in the United States and has been in clinical leadership roles since 2016.He also has an interest in literacy and education. Of particular interest to him is the education of the girl child.

He beilieves Dr. Charles Duncan McIver's motto "If you educate a man, you educate an individual; if educate a woman, you educate a family". He has been supporting education India for more than 15 years and has been working with us since 2021. His contributions include helping with communication, social media, overall strategy and providing feedback on decisions made by the team.
