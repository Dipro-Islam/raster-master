Tapas Mondal

Researcher of “Teacher of the Street”

Our Researcher

Tapas Mondal is a dedicated Indian researcher at Raster Master, specializing in educational development and innovative learning methodologies. With minimal formal academic background but extensive community engagement, practical experience, and a strong passion for community empowerment, Tapas focuses on creating impactful research that addresses the challenges faced by marginalized groups in education.

His work involves exploring effective strategies to enhance teaching practices and learning outcomes, particularly in underrepresented communities. Committed to promoting inclusive education that meets diverse learning needs, he strives to ensure every student has the opportunity to succeed. Guided and mentored by Chief Researcher Deep Narayan Nayak.

Tapas collaborates with fellow researchers and educators to implement evidence-based frameworks that foster lifelong learning and sustainable growth. His research initiatives are driven by a desire to create positive social change and improve educational access for all, making him a significant contributor to Raster Master’s mission of transforming education and uplifting communities while leaving a lasting impact on learners' lives.
