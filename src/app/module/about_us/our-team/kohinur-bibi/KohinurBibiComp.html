Kohinur Bibi Molla

Researcher of “Teacher of the Street”

Our Researcher

Kohinur Bibi Molla is a dedicated researcher at Raster Master, whose invaluable grassroots experience significantly enriches her work in educational research and development. Despite minimal formal academic training, she has developed deep, practical knowledge through years of active engagement with marginalized communities.

Her research focuses on understanding and addressing the educational challenges faced by rural, marginalized, and minority groups in underserved areas. Driven by a profound commitment to improving learning outcomes, Kohinur offers authentic insights into the unique difficulties these communities face, making her a vital contributor to Raster Master's mission of promoting inclusive and accessible education. Guided by the mentorship of Chief Researcher Deep Narayan Nayak, Kohinur continues to deepen her impactful research.
