Kaberi Ghatak

Researcher of “Teacher of the Street”

Our Researcher

Kaberi Ghatak is a compassionate mother, dedicated teacher, and committed researcher who plays a pivotal role in advancing the mission of *Raster Master*. Guided by the visionary leadership of Research Director Deep Narayan Nayak, Kaberi’s work is rooted in a profound dedication to uplifting marginalized communities through education and intergenerational learning.

Her dual role as both a mother and educator brings unique insight and empathy to her research, bridging academic rigor with a real-world understanding of community dynamics. Kaberi’s research focuses on creating inclusive, culturally resonant learning environments that recognize mothers and grandmothers as essential sources of wisdom and support, aligning with *Raster Master's* vision to break cycles of poverty and illiteracy. She embodies the belief that education rooted in compassion and accessibility can be transformative, offering hope and opportunity to future generations.
