Salini Giri

Researcher of “Teacher of the Street”

Our Researcher

Salini Giri is a psychology student at Delhi University and an emerging independent research scholar with Raster Master, with a special focus on the Raster Master 3G model. With a solid academic foundation in psychology.

Salini is dedicated to exploring the mental health and social challenges faced by marginalized communities. Her research examines the psychological barriers that hinder educational and social development, particularly in underserved populations. Guided by the visionary leadership of Chief Researcher Deep Narayan Nayak, Salini is committed to contributing meaningful research that informs Raster Master's efforts in promoting inclusive education, mental well-being, and community empowerment.
