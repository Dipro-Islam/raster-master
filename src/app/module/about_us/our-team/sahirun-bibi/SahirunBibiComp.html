Sahirun Bibi

Researcher of “Teacher of the Street”

Our Researcher

Sahirun Bibi is a devoted mother and grandmother whose extensive life experience offers her a unique and profound understanding of the challenges faced by marginalized communities, often surpassing that of formal researchers. Her deep-rooted knowledge, shaped by her roles as both a mother and a grandmother, drives her commitment to improving education and well-being for underserved populations.

Guided by the visionary leadership of Chief Researcher Deep Narayan Nayak, Sahirun contributes valuable insights and practical solutions that address the needs of rural and marginalized communities. Her research work continues to have a significant impact on advancing inclusive and accessible education.
