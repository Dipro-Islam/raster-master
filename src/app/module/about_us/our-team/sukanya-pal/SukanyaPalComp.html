Dr. Sukanya Pal

Researcher of “Teacher of the Street”

Our Researcher

Dr. Sukanya Pal is an IB practitioner with a strong foundation in psychology and epistemology, dedicated to creating impactful, research-driven educational models for Raster Master under the leadership and guidance of Deep Narayan Nayak, Chief Researcher and Director of Raster Master Research and Development.

Her work includes collaborative research publications, the development of innovative frameworks through design thinking, and partnerships with international organizations to expand educational outreach. Driven by socio-emotional empathy and intellectual humility, Dr. Pal integrates diverse perspectives to foster transformative, globally minded learning experiences. Her expertise lies in cultivating educational approaches that inspire personal growth, critical thinking, and a commitment to equity in education, children’s rights, women’s empowerment, and sustainability, ensuring transformative impact across diverse communities.
