Deep Narayan Nayak

Researcher of “Teacher of the Street”

Our Advisor

Deep Narayan Nayak, M.A. (Education), M.S.W., and MBA (currently pursuing from the Valar Institute at Quantic School of Business and Technology, USA), is a distinguished educator, psychologist, epistemologist, researcher, and developer of innovative research models, frameworks, and policies, as well as a social worker dedicated to creating meaningful, positive change.

As Director of Research, Development, Innovation, Impact, Policy, and Strategic Initiatives at Raster Master, Deep leads pioneering efforts that bridge education, social work, women’s empowerment, gender equality, peace, and community development, with a strong commitment to fostering equitable and inclusive societies. With advanced degrees in education and social work, his expertise spans educational research and development, sustainable and transformative models, policy, social work, community development, and strategic leadership. Under his visionary guidance, more than 100 "Raster Master Grassroots Researchers" are pursuing their research across multiple dimensions, inspired by his practical experience and focus on evidence-based frameworks that promote lifelong learning and sustainable growth within marginalized communities.

Through a holistic approach, Deep advocates for policies and practices that enhance social welfare and empower communities, fostering collaboration and promoting sustainable, transformative impact across diverse sectors.
