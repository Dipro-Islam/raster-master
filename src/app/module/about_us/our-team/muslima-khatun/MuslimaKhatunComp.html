Muslima Khatun

Researcher of “Teacher of the Street”

Our Researcher

Muslima Khatun, a committed researcher at Raster Master, brings a wealth of grassroots experience to her work, offering profound insights into the educational challenges faced by marginalized communities.

Though her formal academic background is limited, her extensive community involvement as both a mother and a researcher provides her with a practical understanding of these communities’ needs. Guided by Chief Researcher Deep Narayan Nayak, Muslima focuses on developing real-world solutions that have a direct, positive impact on underserved populations. Her dedication and firsthand knowledge make her an invaluable part of Raster Master's mission for inclusive, transformative education.
