Soumayan Biswas

Researcher of “Teacher of the Street”

Our Researcher

Soumayan Biswas is a passionate documentary photographer, independent researcher, and social worker dedicated to illuminating social issues and enhancing community well-being. Through his photography, he tells powerful stories that highlight the challenges faced by marginalized communities, aiming to raise awareness and motivate change.

As an independent researcher, Soumayan conducts grassroots research inspired by the work of Deep Narayan Nayak, driven by a desire to understand social dynamics and promote positive transformation. His commitment to social welfare compels him to collaborate with local organizations to develop and implement sustainable solutions for those in need. Guided by the visionary leadership of Deep Narayan Nayak, Soumayan strives to empower communities and craft narratives that resonate widely, fostering understanding and inspiring action.
