Our Workplace

“Teacher of the Street” foundation operates on a global scale, reaching out to communities in need across various regions. Our work areas span continents, and our commitment to making a positive impact on a global scale reflects our belief in the universality of human rights and the need for collective efforts to address challenges. Here's an overview of our work areas:
