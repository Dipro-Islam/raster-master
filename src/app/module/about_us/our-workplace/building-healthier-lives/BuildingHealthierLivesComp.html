Health and Nutrition Center: Building Healthier Lives

Location: Nairobi, Kenya

The Health and Nutrition Center in Nairobi is the backbone of our health and nutrition programs. Here, we develop strategies and coordinate efforts to improve health outcomes in underprivileged communities. Aligned with SDG 3—Good Health and Well-being and SDG 2—Zero Hunger, this center has successfully provided nutritional support to over 15,000 children and healthcare services to more than 25,000 individuals. Our Nairobi team works on initiatives such as maternal and child health programs, community nutrition education, and disease prevention campaigns, ensuring that everyone has access to the care and nutrition they need to thrive.
