Empowering Communities Through
Education, Health, and Hope

Our achievements at Teacher of the Streets are the result of years of dedicated work, driven by a mission to create lasting change in the most vulnerable communities. We are proud to have directly impacted the lives of over 50,000 individuals through our various programs, from education and health to nutrition and gender equality.

Aligned with the UN’s SDGs, we have made significant strides in advancing SDG 4—Quality Education, by increasing literacy rates by 40% in the communities we serve. Our health initiatives, supporting SDG 3—Good Health and Well-being, have reduced maternal and child mortality rates by 25% and provided essential healthcare services to over 20,000 individuals. Additionally, our environmental programs have contributed to the planting of over 100,000 trees, supporting SDG 15—Life on Land. "The measure of achievement is not winning awards. It’s how well we fulfill our mission to reach our goals."

"Every life changed, every mind educated, and every tree planted is a victory worth celebrating."
